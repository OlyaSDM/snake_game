    Control:

    1. Snake Movement: Change the direction of the snake using the arrow keys. You can play on your tablet and phone, buttons will appear on the screen.

    Game Settings:

    1. Food Count: Initially set to 5; increases by 5 when moving to the next level.

    2. Periods: The game consists of 3 levels, after which it will end.

    3. Score Storage: Scores are saved, limited to the last 10 games.

    4. Sounds: A sound is played when food is eaten.

    Game Over:

    1. The game ends if the snake collides with the boundary or itself.

    2. Upon game completion, the final score is displayed and the results are saved.
